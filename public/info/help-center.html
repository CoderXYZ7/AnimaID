Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about using AnimaID

Getting Started

How do I access AnimaID?

Go to the login page and enter your credentials provided by your center administrator. If you don't have credentials, contact your center's management team.

What browsers are supported?

AnimaID works best with modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

How do I reset my password?

Contact your center administrator to reset your password. They will verify your identity and provide you with new login credentials.

Managing Children

How do I check in a child?

Go to the Attendance section, select an event, search for the child by name or registration number, and click the check-in button.

How do I view attendance records?

In the Attendance section, click "View Records" to see historical attendance data with filtering and search capabilities.

How do I add a new child?

Navigate to the Children section and click "Add Child". Fill in all required information including parent contact details and emergency contacts.

Scheduling and Events

How do I create an event?

Go to the Calendar section, click "Add Event", and fill in all the event details including date, time, location, and participant limits.

How do children register for activities?

Parents can view available activities through the public portal and register their children. Staff members can also register children directly in the system.

How do I edit an existing event?

In the Calendar section, find the event you want to modify, click the edit button (pencil icon), make your changes, and save.

Staff Management

How do I manage staff roles?

Administrators can manage staff roles and permissions through the user management section in the admin panel.

How do I assign shifts to staff?

Use the staff coordination features to assign and manage work shifts, ensuring proper coverage for all activities.

Communications

How do I send messages to parents?

Use the Communications section to send individual messages or broadcast announcements to parents and families.

How do I share media with parents?

Upload photos and videos in the Media Manager and share them through the public portal or direct parent communication.

Technical Issues

The system is running slow

Try clearing your browser cache, close unused tabs, or try a different browser. If the problem persists, contact technical support.

I can't access certain features

Check with your administrator to ensure you have the proper permissions. Some features are restricted based on your role.

My session expired

Log back in with your credentials. Sessions automatically expire after a period of inactivity for security.

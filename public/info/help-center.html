Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, contact your system administrator or use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page (if available).

What are my access permissions?

Your access level depends on your assigned role. Check the dashboard or contact your administrator to see which modules you can access.

How do I report an issue?

Use the contact form below or reach out to your system administrator directly with details about the problem.

Can I access AnimaID on mobile devices?

Yes! AnimaID is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. Staff can also use dedicated mobile apps for certain features like attendance tracking.

How do I change my language preferences?

Use the language selector in the header area. Your preference will be saved automatically for future sessions.

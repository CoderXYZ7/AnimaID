Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

How do I register a new child?

Staff members with appropriate permissions can register new children through the Children section in the dashboard. Fill in all required information and upload any necessary documents.

How do I view attendance records?

Attendance records can be viewed in the Attendance section of the dashboard. You can filter by date range, child, or activity.

How do I update activity schedules?

Activity schedules are managed through the Calendar section. Coordinators and administrators can create, edit, or cancel activities as needed.

How do I access the public portal?

The public portal is accessible at /public.html and allows families to view activity information, register for events, and manage their information without requiring a staff account.

Privacy Policy

How we protect and manage your personal information

Last updated: November 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide to us, such as:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Account credentials and preferences
  • Children's information for registration and management
  • Documents and files uploaded to the system

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our platform:

  • IP addresses and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device information and screen resolution

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Process registrations and manage accounts
  • Communicate with you about updates and features
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery

5. Children's Privacy

For children's information stored in our system:

  • Data is encrypted and access-controlled
  • Only authorized staff can view sensitive information
  • Parent/guardian consent is required for data collection
  • Data retention follows local privacy laws

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing in certain circumstances
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and language settings
  • Maintain your session security
  • Analyze website usage and performance
  • Provide personalized features

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account data: Retained while account is active
  • Children's records: As required by local regulations
  • Communication logs: Typically 2-3 years
  • Anonymous analytics: Indefinitely

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including:

  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Your explicit consent where required

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Email notification
  • Notice on our website
  • In-app notifications

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@animaid.com
  • Address: [Global Support Center]

GDPR Compliance: For users in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Contact us to exercise your GDPR rights.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 20, 2025

Introduction

AnimaID ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our animation center management platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Staff member names, contact information, and role assignments
  • Children and family member information collected for registration and safety purposes
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • Contact information for support requests

Usage Data

  • Activity attendance records
  • System access logs and timestamps
  • Communication records within the platform
  • Document and media file management logs

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our animation center management services
  • To manage user accounts and permissions
  • To track attendance and activity participation
  • To facilitate communication between staff, families, and administration
  • To ensure child safety and regulatory compliance
  • To provide technical support and customer service
  • To improve our services and develop new features

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or regulatory requirements
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public
  • In connection with a business transfer or acquisition

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption of sensitive data, secure authentication systems, and regular security audits.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Children's information may be retained for legal and safety purposes according to local regulations regarding child protection records.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to delete your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability

Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy. Information about children is collected only for legitimate educational and safety purposes, with appropriate parental consent. We comply with all applicable children's privacy laws, including COPPA in the United States.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through our platform or by email. Your continued use of AnimaID after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
